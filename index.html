About The Book

Do you have questions of where you will be after death? Are you uncertain of making the rapture?

Heaven, how do people get there?
If these questions trouble you, this book has the perfect answers for you. This book certainly has all the help you, your family and even your ministry needs.

Discover the qualifications for heaven, know what it takes to get there with great expository teaching from this book.

Heaven and Hell Are Realities

Heaven and Hell are not figments of our imaginations or stories told to children to make them conform to certain behavioural patterns, or even make them imbibe certain moral code of conduct. Heaven is real, Hell is real, Various references in the Bible, the word of God, attest to that.


Jesus's story in Luke 16:19 - 27 establishes the existence of life after death for both the sinners and believers. Believers no longer go to Abraham bosom like Lazarus but Heaven, Jesus made this possible by resurrection from the dead. According to his words, where He is, there shall those who believe in Him be also, but the sinners go to Hell.

About the Author

Pastor Isah Mohammed David is the President and Founder of Inter-Denominational Fellowship Ministry (IDF) in Lokoja, Kogi State, Nigeria, where believers are taught the life style of Faith that they might enjoy their inheritance in Christ Jesus.
He is a graduate of Kenneth E. Hagin's Rhema Bible School and also a serving Pastor with Father's Delight Ministry Intl. Zaria.
He is a seasoned minister of the gospel of Christ with a strong teaching Grace.
